About Us

Finding a Home for Laika™ is a platform dedicated to connecting dogs in need with loving families. We facilitate responsible rehoming and adoption processes, ensuring the best outcomes for both pets and their new families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a compassionate community that supports the rehoming of dogs, with a focus on senior and special needs pets. We strive to:

Facilitate responsible pet rehoming and adoption. Educate the public on pet care and the importance of adoption. Support pet owners facing difficult decisions. Collaborate with rescues and shelters to increase adoption rates.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every dog has a loving home. Our goals include:

Reducing the number of pets in shelters. Promoting adoption as the first choice for adding a pet to the family. Creating a support network for pet owners and adopters. Fostering a community that values the human-animal bond.
