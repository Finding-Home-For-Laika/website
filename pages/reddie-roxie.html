Happy Tail: Reddie and Roxxy's Journey to a New Home

This is the heartwarming story of Reddie and Roxxy, two beautiful cattle dogs who had the best life since they were puppies. They were part of a loving family with their dad, Javier, and mom, Cynthia, living in a dream home with a laid-back lifestyle. Nine years ago, everything changed when Javier was diagnosed with cancer. Despite a long, brave battle, he sadly passed away.

The year following Javier's passing was filled with deep sorrow for both Reddie, Roxxy, and Cynthia. She did everything she could to help them cope with the loss, but the pain of living in a home without Javier became too much to bear. Realizing that she could no longer provide the care they deserved, she made the difficult decision to find a new home for them, wishing only the best for her beloved dogs.

After exploring many options, the perfect solution came from within the family. Javier’s nephew and niece, who cherished the memory of their uncle, each decided to take in one of the dogs. Now, Reddie and Roxxy are living their best lives, still close to each other and their family. Whenever the family gathers, the dogs reunite, sharing the same bond they've always had.

Both dogs continue to live in the city where they were born and raised, close to their dad in spirit. As for their mom, she returned to her home country of Lima, Peru, to be with her parents and focus on healing her heart and soul after Javier's passing. She remains in contact with Reddie and Roxxy’s new families, taking comfort in knowing they are well cared for and happy in their new homes.
