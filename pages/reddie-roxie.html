Meet Reddie and Roxie

This adorable pair is eager to find their forever home.

Reddie

  • Large (68 lbs)
  • Male
  • Chill, loves to relax
  • Loyal and fantastic listener
  • Adorable when engaged with "baby talk"

Roxie

  • Large (53 lbs)
  • Female
  • Sweet and attentive
  • Follows you everywhere
  • Energetically drinks water
  • Breed: Cattle dog mixes
  • Location: San Antonio, TX (Owner will pay for relocation within the US)
  • Reddie and Roxie are companion dogs who prefer a relaxed, sedentary life and thrive in a cozy home.
  • If you’re seeking two loyal friends who enjoy spending time with you—whether it’s curling up with a book, watching TV, or just chilling at home—then this duo is perfect for you.
  • They lead a serene and laid-back existence, having spent most of their time together as companions.
  • They are used to living without other pets, prefer being gently guided to their designated areas at home, and value the company of their humans without demanding it constantly.
  • They find comfort in each other’s presence and happily keep each other company throughout the day.
  • They show good eating habits with no signs of food possessiveness and complement each other perfectly, creating a calm and easygoing atmosphere.
  • They are good with other animals, having had contact with dogs, cats, and children. They are also experienced around horses.
