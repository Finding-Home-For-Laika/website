About Us

At Finding a Home for Laika, our mission is to connect senior dogs with loving families. We believe that every dog deserves a chance to live out their golden years in a caring and supportive environment.

Our Story

Inspired by the stories of dogs like Reddie and Roxxy, we set out to create a platform that not only helps dogs find homes but also supports pet owners who, for various reasons, need assistance rehoming their beloved pets.

What We Do

  • Facilitate the adoption of senior dogs.
  • Provide resources and support for pet owners.
  • Collaborate with local rescues and shelters.
  • Promote responsible pet ownership and rehoming.

Join Us

Whether you're looking to adopt, volunteer, or support our cause through donations, there are many ways to get involved. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of these wonderful dogs.
